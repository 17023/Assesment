HOW TO USE A BUTTON.




Desktop/Laptop: Simply use your mouse or mousepad to move your arrow over the button, once you've done this your arrow should change to a pointer, then with left button on your mouse click it once, if this doesn't work try clicking once again then it should work.


Phone/Tablet: Use your finger to click the middle of the button, if this doesn't work try clicking the button once again, then it should work.
