Covid Tracer

This app can be downloaded on the App Store and the Google Play Store. It is a way of tracking your whereabouts so that it if you get infected by Covid-19 the government can trace any close contacts. They also use this app to let you know if you have come in contact with anyone that has Covid-19.

Help.

Clicking "Guide" will bring up a detailed explanation of how this app works. There are four sections of instructions including recording vists, bluetooth tracing, contact alerts and retrace your steps. There is a large "Next" button to navigate through the pages, and if you want to need to go back to a previous page click the left arrow at the top of the page. Clicking the "X" at the top right will close the page and bring you back to the page shown in the image.


Diary.

Your diary shows all places you have scanned into for the past two weeks. To add an entry to a previous date click "Add diary entry", this will bring you to same page as if you were to manually enter a vist.

Place or activity: Clicking this will bring you to a new page where you can add the location of place you visted. You also have the option to say you stayed home or choose a location that you have saved previously.

Arrival date & Time: This date is automatically entered as today, however you can change it by clicking it.

Details: This is an optional part, however, it is helpful to full it out if you were with someone else especially if they don't have access to the NZ COVID TRACER APP.


Camera.

This is where your camera will appear, it will not be black. When you're trying to scan the QR Code try to align it in between the four corners. If the camera is not picking up the QR Code you can try turning on the torch, which is located below the right corner. Below is an example of a QR Code.

This QR Code will bring you to the Ministry Of Healths offical website, explaining more on the COVID TRACER APP.


Manual Diary.

If you are unable to scan the code for whatever reason, clicking this gives you the option to enter your vist manually. The following image is what will be displayed when you click it:

Arrival date & Time: This date is automatically entered as today, however you can change it by clicking it.

Details: This is an optional part, however, it is helpful to full it out if you were with someone else especially if they don't have access to the NZ COVID TRACER APP.


Dashboard.

The dashboard is a page of the latest announcements, what's new, tracing and latest updates around COVID-19 in New Zealand and the app. You can also click the "More info" tab at the top, which gives you more infomation on Alert Levels, Contact tracing, Vaccines and help with your mental wellbeing.


Record Vist.

This is the page the loads when you open the app and the one that is displayed in the image. This button allows you to return to this page if your on the Dashboard or Your Data.


My Data.

The My Data page contains multiple things including:

Information stored privately: This includes your diary of vists, saved locations and your NHI number.

Information shared with contact tracers: This allows you to fill in your contact details, that allows the government to contact you sonner if they need to.

If you test positive: This area is important if you test positive for COVID-19, it allows you to share your digital diary and bluetooth tracing codes.

Setting and support: If you need any help, want to give feedback or change your notification preferences it is located under this header.
